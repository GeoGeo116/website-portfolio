I often work on web development in my spare time (hence the website) and I love being able to have my own creative freedom doing so.

  • UI Design - Ensuring that the layout of the site is to a good standard (and dynamic)

I offer these services over on AssureUX as well as doing it for freelance work, so I have plenty of experience.
